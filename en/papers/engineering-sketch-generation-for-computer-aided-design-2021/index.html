Deep Analysis

Innovations

  • CurveGen and TurtleGen: generative models that produce curve primitives without a sketch constraint solver
  • Explicit topology modeling for downstream constraint and 3D CAD operations

Methodology

The paper proposes two generative models, CurveGen and TurtleGen, that generate engineering sketches as curve primitives while explicitly considering topology. The models are evaluated via a perceptual study with human subjects comparing against the state-of-the-art for engineering sketch generation.

Key Results

Both CurveGen and TurtleGen produce more realistic engineering sketches than the current state-of-the-art in a human perceptual evaluation.
