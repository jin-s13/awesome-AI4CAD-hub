Deep Analysis

Innovations

  • Novel data augmentation paradigm that prompts an LLM to generate CAD programs conditioned on a reference surface program and a modeling procedure, inspired by industrial design practices.
  • Use of a collection of organic shapes as reference surfaces to introduce spline-based curvature edges and faces, enriching geometric diversity and addressing underrepresentation in existing datasets.

Methodology

The method prompts a Large Language Model with a reference surface program and a modeling procedure to generate CAD programs. By varying the reference surface using a set of organic shapes, it produces diverse CAD models that include spline-based curvature features. Experiments evaluate geometric diversity and resemblance to industry-grade designs.

Key Results

Generated CAD samples exhibit significantly greater geometric diversity and a higher proportion of organic shape primitives, making them more similar to industry-grade CAD designs.
