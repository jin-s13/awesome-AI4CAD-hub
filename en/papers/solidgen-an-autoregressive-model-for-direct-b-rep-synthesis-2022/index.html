Deep Analysis

Innovations

  • Direct B-rep synthesis without requiring CAD modeling sequence supervision
  • SolidGen: autoregressive model predicting vertices, edges, and faces using Transformer and pointer networks
  • Indexed Boundary Representation: hierarchical referencing of B-rep elements to capture geometric and topological relations for machine learning
  • Probabilistic modeling of B-rep distribution enabling conditioning on class labels, images, and voxels

Methodology

SolidGen is an autoregressive neural network that directly models B-rep by predicting vertices, edges, and faces sequentially using Transformer-based and pointer neural networks. It uses a novel Indexed Boundary Representation that organizes B-rep elements in a hierarchy to capture geometric and topological relations. The model is trained on B-rep data without CAD modeling sequence supervision and can be conditioned on various contexts.

Key Results

SolidGen produces high-quality, realistic CAD models as shown by qualitative, quantitative, and human perceptual evaluations.
