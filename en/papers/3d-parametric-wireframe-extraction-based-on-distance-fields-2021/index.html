Deep Analysis

Innovations

  • Parametric wireframe extraction from point clouds via scalar distance fields representing proximity to sharp feature curves
  • Pipeline integrating corner detection, curve segmentation, topological graph construction, and spline fitting
  • Output of editable parametric spline curves that can be sampled arbitrarily

Methodology

The method processes a scalar distance field computed from densely sampled point clouds to capture proximity to sharp feature curves. It then detects corners, constructs curve segmentation, builds a topological graph fitted to the wireframe, and finally produces parametric spline curves. Evaluation is performed on 50 complex 3D shapes, comparing against a deep learning-based technique.

Key Results

The approach demonstrates superior quality compared to a novel deep learning-based method on 50 complex 3D shapes.
