Deep Analysis

Innovations

  • Robust primitive extraction by iteratively estimating the probability distribution of noise to eliminate outliers
  • Intersection-aware constraints (tangency and collinearity) to correctly obtain intersections between primitives

Methodology

The method robustly extracts primitives by iteratively estimating noise probability distribution to remove outliers, then applies intersection-aware constraints such as tangency and collinearity to derive correct primitive intersections, constructing a valid B-rep solid. It is evaluated against classical and learning-based baselines on reconstruction efficiency and accuracy.

Key Results

The proposed method outperforms both classical and recent learning-based methods in reconstruction efficiency and accuracy, producing valid B-rep models without extensive manual topology correction.
